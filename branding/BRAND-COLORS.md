# Uptime Hive - Brand Colors

## Primary Colors

### Amber (Primary Brand Color)
- **Light**: `#fbbf24` - RGB(251, 191, 36)
- **Medium**: `#f59e0b` - RGB(245, 158, 11)
- **Dark**: `#d97706` - RGB(217, 119, 6)

### Slate (Text & Dark Elements)
- **Dark**: `#1e293b` - RGB(30, 41, 59)
- **Medium**: `#334155` - RGB(51, 65, 85)
- **Light**: `#64748b` - RGB(100, 116, 139)

### White
- **Pure**: `#ffffff` - RGB(255, 255, 255)
- **Off-White**: `#f8fafc` - RGB(248, 250, 252)

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo on Dark Backgrounds
- Use `icon.svg` or `wordmark-dark-bg.svg`
- Hexagon: `#fbbf24` with `#f59e0b` stroke
- Text: White/Off-White for "uptime", Amber for "hive"

### Logo on Light Backgrounds
- Use `icon-light-bg.svg` or `wordmark-light-bg.svg`
- Hexagon: `#f59e0b` with `#d97706` stroke
- Text: Slate Dark for "uptime", Amber Medium for "hive"

### Monochrome Usage
- Dark mono: Use on light backgrounds
- Light mono: Use on dark backgrounds
- Acceptable for single-color print, watermarks, etc.

## Minimum Sizes
- Icon: 16px minimum (favicon), 32px recommended
- Wordmark: 120px width minimum
- Stacked: 80px width minimum

## Clear Space
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the pulse line around all sides of the logo
